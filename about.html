About AutoDigiX

AI Automation & Performance Marketing That Drives Real Business Growth

Our Mission

AutoDigiX is a performance-focused AI Automation and Digital Marketing agency dedicated to helping businesses scale intelligently in today’s competitive digital landscape. We combine strategic marketing frameworks with automation systems that eliminate inefficiencies and maximize revenue potential. Digital marketing allows businesses to reach targeted audiences through data-driven campaigns across Google, Meta, search engines, and social platforms. Instead of guesswork, we use analytics, conversion tracking, and performance metrics to continuously optimize campaigns for better ROI. AI automation enhances this growth by streamlining repetitive processes, improving lead nurturing systems, automating follow-ups, and building scalable workflows. Together, marketing and automation create predictable growth, stronger brand authority, and long-term business sustainability.

Founder – Praveen Gorla

Praveen Gorla is the founder of AutoDigiX and a performance marketing strategist driven by a clear mission — to help businesses grow intelligently through data, automation, and structured digital systems. With hands-on experience in Google Ads, Meta Ads, SEO, and AI-powered workflows, he has worked closely with brands to build scalable marketing engines that produce measurable, consistent results. Rather than focusing on vanity metrics, Praveen emphasizes revenue-driven performance, conversion optimization, and long-term brand positioning. His approach blends analytical precision with strategic creativity — ensuring that every campaign, automation system, and funnel is aligned with real business growth objectives. Through AutoDigiX, he aims to empower businesses with modern digital infrastructure — combining performance marketing and AI automation to create predictable growth, operational efficiency, and sustainable scalability.
